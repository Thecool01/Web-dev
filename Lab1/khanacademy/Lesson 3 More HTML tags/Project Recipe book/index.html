Curd casserole with semolina

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 6 person
Ingredients Quantity
Cottage cheese 300g
Egg 1 ptc
Milk 60ml
Semolina 30g
Sugar 1.5 tbsp. spoons (to taste)
Salt 1 pinch
Butter (for greasing the mold) 1 teaspoon

Step 1: Pour milk over semolina in a bowl.
Step 2: Mix semolina with milk and set aside for a while. While we are making cottage cheese, the semolina will swell slightly.
Step 3: For this casserole, the cottage cheese must be ground through a fine metal sieve. Do it in parts. The softer and fattier the cottage cheese, the easier it is to grind it. This step is very important for a perfectly tender casserole, so don't skip it.
Step 4: This is what the cottage cheese will look like after grinding - airy, similar to coconut shavings.
Step 5: Turn on the oven and preheat to 170 degrees. Add semolina with milk to the grated cottage cheese.
Step 6: Beat the egg here, add sugar and salt.
Step 7: Stir until a homogeneous curd mass is obtained.
Step 8: Grease the sides and bottom of the baking dish with butter. Place the curd mixture into the prepared pan and smooth the surface.
Step 9: Cook the cottage cheese casserole in a preheated oven at 170 degrees for 45 minutes to 1 hour, until golden brown.
Step 10: Let the finished casserole cool. After this, it will hold its shape when cut. Enjoy your meal!

Millet porridge

  • Time: 30 min
  • Serves: 4 person
Ingredients Quantity
Millet 1 glass
Water or milk 2-2,5 glasses / 2-3 glasses
Butter 2-3 tbsp. spoons
Salt 0.25-0.5 teaspoons
Sugar (optional) 1.5 tbsp. spoons (to taste)
Salt 1 pinch
Butter (for greasing the mold) 1-3 tablespoons

Porridge with water

Step 1: Before preparing millet porridge, it is advisable to choose cereals that are more yellow in color. So, here are the products you will need to prepare millet porridge.
Step 2: Millet cereals must be thoroughly washed with water (5-7 times) and scalded with boiling water.
Step 3: Place the cereal in boiling salted water, add 1 tbsp. spoon of oil and, stirring, cook the porridge over low heat until thickened. If you cook the porridge over too high a heat, the water may boil away before the porridge is ready (15-20 minutes). In this case, you need to add 0.5 cups of hot boiled water.
Step 4: When serving, add the remaining melted butter to the millet porridge and stir.

Porridge with milk

Step 1: The prepared cereal must first be boiled in 1-2 glasses of salted water until half cooked, 5-10 minutes (if the cereal is cooked in a large amount of water, drain off the remaining water). Boil the milk.
Step 2: After this, pour in hot milk, add butter and sugar.
Step 3: Cook the porridge until thickened. Then the millet porridge can be put in the oven.
Step 4: You can serve millet milk porridge with fruit or chocolate.
Step 5: For liquid millet porridge, take 4-5 glasses of water or milk for 1 glass of cereal. Enjoy your meal!

Homemade cupcakes with milk

  • Time: 40 minutes
  • Serves: 12 pcs.
Ingredients Quantity
Butter 120g
Eggs 2 pcs.
Sugar 200g
Flour 220g
Baking powder 5g
Vanilla extract or vanilla sugar 1 teaspoon

Step 1: To bake cupcakes we will need molds and paper cups.
Step 2: Mix semolina with milk and set aside for a while. While we are making cottage cheese, the semolina will swell slightly.
Step 3: Sift 220 g of flour with 5 g of baking powder into a bowl (you can sift the flour 2 times).
Step 4: Place 120g soft butter in a mixer bowl. Beat until the butter turns white. Without stopping whisking, add 200 g of sugar. Continue beating some more.
Step 5: Continuing to beat, add 2 eggs and 5 g of vanilla sugar one by one. Beat until the mixture turns white. While beating, remove the mixture from the sides and stir until the dough is homogeneous.
Step 6: Then the mixer rotation speed should be reduced to minimum, add flour and 120 ml of milk. Flour and milk should be added one at a time in three additions.
Step 7: Using a pastry bag, mechanical ice cream scoop, or regular tablespoon, scoop the batter into paper cups (they should be 2/3 full).
Step 8: Bake cupcakes in a preheated oven. The temperature at which cupcakes need to be baked is 180Â°C. Time - approximately 20 minutes. You can check readiness with a wooden stick. Remove the finished cupcakes from the oven, let them cool slightly, then place them on a wire rack or board without removing them from the paper cups and let them cool completely.
Step 9: Homemade milk cupcakes are ready. Ready baked goods can be decorated with cream.
